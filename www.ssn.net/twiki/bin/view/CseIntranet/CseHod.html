HOD's Desk

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) was established in the year 1996 with the mission to educate students from all over India and other countries, including those from rural areas, so that they become enlightened individuals, improving the living standards of their Families, Industry and Society.

CSE shares the mission and vision of the institute in imparting high quality education to the students. First, it is ensured that the student perceives the computer fundamentals thoroughly. Besides fulfilling the requirements of the syllabus prescribed by the University, the program offers students the opportunity to work on diverse hardware and software platforms.

The Department, accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) in the year 2003, has organized three AICTE-ISTE Short Term Training Programmes, funded by the AICTE and three Faculty Development Programmes sponsored by Anna University as well as one International Conference with all the departments of SSN College of Engineering. It has organized a national conference on “Medical Informatics” funded by the management and has been organizing many symposia, guest lectures and CSI & ISTE student Chapters. The Department is well equipped with 10 diversified laboratories including a dedicated laboratory for the Post-Graduate students installed with a variety of software catering the need of the students and covering the curriculum specified by the affiliated University. The laboratories include Operating System and Networking Lab, Systems Programming Lab, Software Engineering Lab, Windows Programming Lab, Multimedia and Graphics Lab, Java Technology Lab, Hardware Lab I, Hardware Lab II, Post-Graduate (PG) Lab and High Performance Computing Lab.

The department consistently earns ranks and recognitions from the University. So far 91% of the final year undergraduate students have been placed in various reputed companies through campus recruitment process.
