GE 8291 ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

Course Instructor- Dr. A. Murugesan

Syllabus:

PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES:

Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering curriculum is designed to impart Knowledge, Skill and Attitude on the graduates to

1. Have a successful career in Mechanical Engineering and allied industries.

2. Have expertise in the areas of Design, Thermal, Materials and Manufacturing.

3. Contribute towards technological development through academic research and industrial practices.

4. Practice their profession with good communication, leadership, ethics and social responsibility.

5. Graduates will adapt to evolving technologies through life-long learning.
